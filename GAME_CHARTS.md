| Roll | Yards | Roll | Yards | Roll | Yards | Roll | Yards |
|------|-------|------|-------|------|-------|------|-------|
| 0    | -10   | 5    | 2     | 10   | 4     | 15   | 10    |

| 3    | 1     | 8    | 3     | 13   | 7     | 18   | 30    |
| 4    | 1     | 9    | 4     | 14   | 8     | 19   | TD    |

**Results:**
- **TD or reaches end zone:** Touchdown! 6 pts + extra point attempt
- **Yards gained ≥ yards to go:** First down! Keep ball, start new drive
- **Otherwise:** Turnover on downs, opponent gets ball

## Extra Point Conversions

### 1-Point Conversion (~85% success)
- Roll d20 on Field Goal table above
- **Success if Make Distance ≥ 15 yards**
- Success = 7 total points (6+1)
- Failure = 6 total points

### 2-Point Conversion (40% success)
- Roll d10 (0-9)
- **Success on 6 or higher**
- Success = 8 total points (6+2)
- Failure = 6 total points

## Field Position
- **0 yards** = Bombers' goal line (Bombers advance toward 100)
- **100 yards** = Gunners' goal line (Gunners advance toward 0)

## Turnover Summary

| Play Style | Turnover Rolls | Chance |
|------------|----------------|--------|
| Run-First  | 0              | 5%     |
| Balanced   | 0-1            | 10%    |
| Pass-First | 0-2            | 15%    |

**If turnover occurs:**
- Drive ends immediately, no punt/FG
- If TD result: opponent gets ball at their 20
- Otherwise: opponent gets ball at current spot

## Drive Sequence Checklist

1. ☐ Choose play style (Balanced / Run / Pass)
2. ☐ Roll d20 for drive outcome (yards & time)
3. ☐ Roll d20 for turnover check
4. ☐ Apply yards, check for safety
5. ☐ Check for touchdown
6. ☐ If neither, determine 4th down distance
7. ☐ Decide: Go for it / Field Goal / Punt
8. ☐ Subtract time from clock
